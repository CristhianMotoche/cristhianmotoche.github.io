# Macroid is...
Is a DSL for Android + Scala projects

# Concepts
- Bricks ->

  - widget
- Tweaks ->
  - something that changes a property of a widget.

# Why I like Macroid?
- Tweaks and Bricks are composable
- You can define your bricks anywhere
